What Happens Next

Four simple steps from here to live on both stores.

1

Activate the Bookly API Addon

Purchase and install one small addon on your existing WordPress site. This is what lets the app talk to your Bookly database. We handle the installation and setup.

~£60 one-time (CodeCanyon)
2

Register as an Apple Developer

Needed to publish on the Apple App Store. Simple online enrolment via developer.apple.com.

£79 / year
3

Register on Google Play Console

Needed to publish on the Android Play Store. One-time registration at play.google.com/console.

£20 one-time
4

Submit to Both Stores

App icons, screenshots, and store listings are prepared and submitted. Apple typically reviews in 1–3 days, Google Play in 1–2 days.

Included in development
