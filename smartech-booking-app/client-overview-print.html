What Happens Next
Four simple steps from here to live on both stores.

Purchase and install one small addon on your existing WordPress site. This is what lets the app talk to your Bookly database. We handle the full installation and setup for you.
~£60 one-time · CodeCanyon

Register as an Apple Developer
Required to publish on the Apple App Store. Simple online enrolment via developer.apple.com — takes around 15 minutes.

£20 one-time
